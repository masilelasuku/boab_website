About Us

A Trusted Partner in Construction

BOAB Investments (PTY) LTD is a leading construction company based in Manzini, Eswatini, dedicated to delivering exceptional building solutions that stand the test of time.

With a steadfast commitment to quality workmanship, integrity, and safety, we have earned the trust of clients across residential, commercial, and civil sectors.
